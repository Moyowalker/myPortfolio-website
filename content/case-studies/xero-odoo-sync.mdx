## Challenge
Growing teams relied on both Xero and Odoo but lacked a trustworthy integration path between them. Manual exports created mismatched balances, currency errors, and unnecessary compliance risk.

## Approach
- Modeled canonical financial objects and reconciliation rules with finance stakeholders before implementing the sync layer.
- Built a message-driven Python service with resilient retry behavior and explicit failure handling.
- Used Odoo XML-RPC and the Xero SDK for delta-based updates, guarded with hashing, auditability, and idempotent processing.
- Added monitoring and reporting layers so finance operators could understand state quickly and recover from issues without guesswork.

## Outcome
- Delivered &lt;15 minute delay across ledgers with near-zero duplicate postings.
- Cut reconciliation cycles from days to under two hours.
- Produced auditable trails for regulatory reporting and simplified external audits.
