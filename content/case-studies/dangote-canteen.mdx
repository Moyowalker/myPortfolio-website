## Challenge
High-volume canteen operations tend to rely on manual coordination, verbal handoffs, and reporting that arrives too late to improve service in the moment. That creates avoidable friction for supervisors and weak visibility for leadership.

## Approach
- Mapped the full operating flow from planning through issuance, internal checkpoints, exceptions, and reporting.
- Structured the product around service states and operational checkpoints instead of generic admin screens.
- Prioritized in-the-moment visibility for supervisors so service issues could be identified while operations were still active.
- Designed the interface to reduce ambiguity for the people executing the workflow daily, not just the stakeholders reviewing summaries later.

## Outcome
- Created a more legible operational view of canteen activity across planning, service, and reporting.
- Reduced dependence on ad hoc coordination for day-to-day service execution.
- Established a stronger foundation for accountability, reporting quality, and continuous process improvement.
