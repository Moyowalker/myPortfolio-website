## Challenge
Logistics systems become fragile when movement planning, status updates, and operational decisions are spread across too many manual channels. In supply-linked businesses, that becomes expensive very quickly.

## Approach
- Started with the coordination model: what must be visible, who needs to act, and which decisions have to happen quickly.
- Structured the platform around movement states, execution checkpoints, and operational updates instead of disconnected task lists.
- Designed for clarity over noise so operators can recognize exceptions without wading through clutter.
- Built the product direction to support growth from core execution infrastructure into broader logistics intelligence over time.

## Outcome
- Established a stronger digital backbone for logistics execution and operational visibility.
- Improved the ability to reason about workflow state instead of chasing fragmented updates.
- Created room for scale without simply preserving manual chaos in software form.
