## Challenge
The artisan market has a trust problem. Customers often cannot verify quality or reliability, while skilled artisans struggle to present credibility in a way that converts into real work.

## Approach
- Treated trust as the product core rather than something added after the marketplace exists.
- Designed for stronger credibility signals, higher-quality profiles, and lower uncertainty during selection.
- Considered the needs of both sides of the marketplace so confidence grows symmetrically, not only for customers.
- Focused on making the experience feel safe, legible, and useful before chasing feature volume.

## Outcome
- Defined a stronger trust model for customer-artisan interactions.
- Turned credibility into a visible product mechanic instead of a vague brand promise.
- Built a foundation for a service marketplace where reliability can become a measurable competitive edge.
