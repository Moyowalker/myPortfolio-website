## Challenge
Asset-heavy organizations lose control quickly when ownership, movement, and condition tracking are spread across spreadsheets, email chains, and inconsistent internal processes. The result is weak governance and difficult audits.

## Approach
- Treated the platform as a governance system rather than a passive register of assets.
- Modeled assignment, transfer, returns, and accountability as first-class workflow states.
- Focused the interface on operational confidence: who currently owns what, what changed, and where action is required.
- Balanced control-heavy requirements with a UI teams could realistically adopt without bypassing the process.

## Outcome
- Produced a clearer operational record for asset lifecycles and ownership changes.
- Made movement history easier to audit across teams and locations.
- Reduced the friction involved in understanding asset status, accountability, and exceptions.
