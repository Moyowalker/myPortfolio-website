## Challenge
Emocom needed a single operational layer to coordinate OEM engagements, partner enablement, customer delivery, and internal handoffs. Existing global tools were expensive and poorly aligned with local operating realities.

## Approach
- Facilitated product discovery with sales, delivery, and finance stakeholders to capture milestones, approvals, and data handoffs.
- Architected a modular service layer with MongoDB and Odoo integrations to support master data, workflow state, and reporting.
- Embedded Automation Anywhere bots for document triage, SLA tracking, and escalation-heavy tasks.
- Delivered dashboards and operational notifications so leadership could monitor progress, identify bottlenecks, and unblock teams faster.

## Outcome
- Improved cross-team visibility, reducing handoff delays by 45%.
- Automated document processing, saving ~30 hours per week across operations leads.
- Established a roadmap for AI copilots to assist with proposal drafting and risk identification.
